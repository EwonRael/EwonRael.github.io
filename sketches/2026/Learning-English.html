INSTRUCTOR

Hola, y enhorabuena por empezar su aventura de aprendizaje del idioma inglés. Durante los próximos tres meses, aprenderá a desenvolverse en conversaciones básicas. Aquí tiene un ejemplo del tipo de conversación que podrá mantener al finalizar este curso:

MARIA

Excuse me sir... Where is the bathroom?

JOSÉ

The bathroom is over there, ma’am.

MARIA

Thank you sir... And the sandwich? Where is the sandwich?

JOSÉ

Here it is miss. I understand. You are Spanish. And you have no time.

MARIA

Thank you sir. You are very friendly. I like to ski, and I have two sisters and one brother.

JOSÉ

You have many siblings, ma’am.

MARIA

That is correct sir. The restaurant, does it have drinks? I like to drink at restaurants with my sandwich.

JOSÉ

Yes ma’am. We have beer, and we have lemonade.

MARIA

Beer is good, but I don’t like lemonade. Is the beer cold or is it hot?

JOSÉ

The beer is cold ma’am. The beer is in the kitchen, and it is very cold.

MARIA

One time I saw a cat, and it was a mother cat. It makes me feel sad.

JOSÉ

Why did the cat make you sad?

MARIA

I don’t know. Maybe I want to be a mother, but the world is very bad. At this moment, I don’t believe I can have children because the world is very bad and sad and it is not a good place to have children.

JOSÉ

Is the world a bad place for cats to have children?

MARIA

I don’t know. Maybe it is true. Maybe I am sad for the small cat who will live in the world, or maybe the world is good for cats. Cats are very stupid. They don’t know how to ski. I like to ski. In the future I want to ski, but the weather is hot. The weather is too hot to ski.

JOSÉ

I understand. The world is very sad. I will bring you a beer. The price is zero. It is free.

MARIA

Thank you sir. You are very friendly, and you work at the restaurant.

JOSÉ

No problem ma’am. I like to work at the restaurant. I wear a white shirt and blue pants.

MARIA

My name is Maria. It is very nice to meet you.

JOSÉ

Likewise. My name is José.

MARIA

Have a good day, José.

JOSÉ

Thank you. Have a good day, Maria.
