ABOUT

If you wish to send a message, to a stranger far,
and you’re thinking of a bottle, or perhaps a jar,
then friend, your transit’s ineffective, there’s better means by now,
the new way you can do from home, if you just know how!

I’ll start by asking you ten questions; the answers should intend
to illuminate your person. Once finished I will send
your answers to the next brave soul, who answers questions true,
and in return I’ll send the prior's answers on to you.
