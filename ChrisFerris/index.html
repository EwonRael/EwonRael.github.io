I am a writer of personal essays which have been published by Literary Mama, Chalkbeat, The Los Angeles Times, and the Bank Street College Occasional Papers Series. I am nearing completion of a memoir about founding and leading a public charter school in Los Angeles. I live in Denver with my husband. My two sons are both in college, one in Kansas City and the other in The Hague. I am the Executive Director of Highline Academy Charter Schools in Denver. I founded and led Our Community School, a K-8 charter school in Los Angeles CA from 2005 to 2013. I am a consultant for multiple charters in Denver, Los Angeles, and nationally.
