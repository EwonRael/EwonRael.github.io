What is art to you?

I think art is kinda just a general term for attaching meaning to things, or some sort of stylization. For instance, things like abstract art or body awareness such as dance and martial arts don't really always have a symbolic nature (though they can), without the intended meaning it's revolving around the sensory aesthetic that may or may not provide meaning or sensation to the viewer and performer. All in all, I think most things are "art". If you say something is art, can you really argue against their perspective?

- Richard Arnold
